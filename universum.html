Noah Brandt

Noah Brandt - German and East Belgian librarian, author and consultant.

Homepage

Librarian, author, and consultant Noah Brandt, born in 2002 in Bonn-Gronau and currently residing there, has extensive professional experience in librarianship. His career includes positions at renowned institutions such as the specialized library of the Friedrich Ebert Foundation. He also gained valuable experience at the media library of the AHS in Eupen, the educational media library in St. Vith, as well as the public parish library in Bütgenbach. In addition, Mr. Brandt works as an author and provides consulting services to libraries. He is also actively engaged in voluntary work in various library institutions.
